How it works
1
📁 Choose how to get started
Upload your bank statement for automatic analysis, or enter your income, expenses, and EMI manually — no statement required.
2
🔍 Review your financial snapshot
Confirm your annual income, expenses, and EMI. Set how many years remain on your loan — the planner will drop EMI automatically after that point.
3
💼 Build your corpus & allocation
Enter your existing portfolio value across 11 asset classes. Set your investment % of income — it gets distributed equally across assets (edit per class if needed).
4
⚙️ Set personal details & assumptions
Enter your current age, retirement age, and life expectancy. Adjust income growth rate, inflation, and expected returns per asset class.
5
📈 Calculate your retirement projection
See a year-by-year table of corpus growth through accumulation, and how long your savings sustain you post-retirement — with an "Out of Money" alert if the corpus runs dry.
🔒 All processing happens locally in your browser — your financial data never leaves your device.
