Financial Planner

Understand your money. Plan your retirement.

Financial Planner is a browser-based, privacy-first tool that brings clarity to your finances. Upload your bank statement to automatically classify transactions into income, expenses, EMIs, and investments — then use the Investment Planner to project your corpus across 11 asset classes using Indian market return assumptions. The planner models wealth growth through the accumulation phase and shows exactly how long your savings will sustain you post-retirement, factoring in inflation, income growth, and configurable EMI tenure. All processing happens locally in your browser — your data never leaves your device.
